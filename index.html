About me

My background includes certifications in UI/UX design (Zero to Mastery) and English proficiency (B2/C1, prooved by EU Academy Certificate), as well as practical experience using AI tools like ChatGPT to speed up development without compromising quality.

My mission is to improve and accelerate the app development process while ensuring code quality and long-term scalability — helping schools and businesses launch digital products that truly support learning.
